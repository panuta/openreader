Wide range file support

Quickly share or view documents witch colleagues without having to think about what type of file it is. All files can be used, without any converting, directly on the iPad. A wide range of document files are supported, including MS Word, MS Excel, txt, MP4, PDF and of course Powerpoint.

Banking industry grade security

Do you trust internet banking? Good! Than you can trust your documents are safe with us. We use the same data encryption that is used in E-banking. Our specific server configuration ensures optimal security. The safety and confidentiality of your company documents is our main priority at all times.

Hierarchical document permission

This platform enables you to give hierarchical document permission. Meaning that some people or groups have permission to different documents than others. Just like you intern now does not see all company files, he won't using the OpenReader platform.

Automatic document sync on all devices

All devices are synchronized with the same company library. Therefore everyone has every file they need available to them. Using and sharing information in this way makes working together in teams so much easier!

Dramatically reduce your environmental impact

It won't come as a surprise that using paper is far from good for our planet, considering it is made from the very things that normally absorb our CO2 emissions.
