FAQ

  • What is Openreader?
    OpenReader is a cloud service designed for businesses. It will help you manage and organize all your company files in one central digital liblary. It makes meetings easier and exchanging files with colleagues a simple task!
  • How can I try Openreader for free?
    The first month you can try it without any costs. We won't even ask for your payment information before you decide whether you want to keep using this platform. If you do however decide to keep it, payment will start immediately. First month included.
  • Can I remove or add users later on?
    You sure can! You can add or remove as many users as you want and it won't even effect your contract length. Payment is always per month and at the end of the month.
  • What is the difference between a normal user and the admin-user?
    The bigs difference is that the admin user, normally someone higher up in the company’s hierarchy, controls the individual document permission. Confidentiality is key in all of OpenReader, in this way you can even ensure this in your company itself.

    The Admin user is also able to add or remove users and organizations.
  • what do I need to use this platform?
    The user needs an Ipad to view and read all of his files. Controlling document permission, adding or removing people and organizations happens on the OpenReader website.
  • How safe is my information?
    From start to end the OpenReader platform has been designed for businesses, a key issue has therefore always been the confidentiality and security of our clients data. We have taken extensive measures to ensure optimal safety. The Security/privacy information next to the FAQ shows more details on what has been done exactly to make it as secure as possible.
  • Which file formats does Openreader support?
    A wide range of file extensions are supported. These are the different types, MS Word, MS Excel, Power point, Pdf, MP3, MP4, MOV, txt. So you can read or view all of the most commonly used files!
  • What happens if I want to stop using it ?
    It depends on the contract you have. If you have an one year contract and you want to terminate it, the remaining fee will have to be paid. This fee is based on the amount of users the contract was signed with at the beginning of the contract and on the amount of months still remaining.
  • How much space do I get?
    As much as you need! There is no fixed limit and you don’t pay for your data usage. There is however a fair use policy, also any sharing of copyright protected material is strictly prohibited. More detailed information on this can be found in the terms of agreement
  • How many users can one account have?
    As much as you want to have. There is no limit to the amount of users. Pricing is however based how many users your company has. So adding or removing users will influence the monthly price you pay. This is something you can change every month, so you have complete freedom in terms of how many users you have.

Privacy / Security information

The OpenReader platform is made for businesses and we acknowledge the fact that they have different needs than consumers. A lot of enterprises handle very delicate and confidential information and therefore security is key. This has been from the very start one of the biggest priorities. Optimized safety of information.
To ensure save file transfer, all your files are transferred using SSL. This creates a secure transfer tunnel protected by AES 256-bit encryption. The files are stored encrypted by the AES 256 standard. This is the same type of encryption that is used by banks to secure their customer data.


The server used by Openreader has ISO 27001 certification, so what does that mean?

  • The server is systematically evaluated for information security risks, taking into account the impact of company threats and vulnerabilities
  • A comprehensive suite of information security controls and other forms of risk management to address company and architectre security risks has been designed and implemented
  • An overarching management process is impelmented to ensure that the information security controls meet the information security needs on an on-going basis
